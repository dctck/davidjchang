Beam Dashboard

Transforming a manual distribution process into a self-service platform that empowers creators while preventing automated abuse.

TIMELINE

The Problem

The Challenge

Creators wanted to distribute digital collectibles to their communities, but our manual process was a bottleneck. They had to submit forms and wait days for our team to create QR codes for distribution.

Our existing creator tool was overly technical, and worse, automated bots made by exploiters were claiming all items before real community members could participate.

User Pain Points

"The approval process takes too long... my event will be over by then"

"The interface is too technical, I don't understand these settings"

"Bots grabbed everything before my real fans could claim anything"

Research & Discovery

Understanding Creator Workflows

Through user interviews, I discovered three main distribution patterns:

70%

Social Media

Shared via Twitter, Telegram to build community

25%

In-person Events

Used at conferences, meetups for on-site engagement

5%

Digital Campaigns

Embedded in websites and marketing

Key Insight

Creators needed both mass distribution capability AND security controls to ensure real people, not bots, received their items.

The Bot Problem

Analysis revealed campaigns were being "swept" by automated claiming within minutes, defeating the purpose of community building.

Security Requirements:

Prevent mass automated claiming (Claim Limits)
Allow targeting specific communities (Locations)
Enable tracking for accountability (Manager/Dashboard

Anti-Abuse Strategy

Instead of complex technical controls, I focused on user-friendly options:

Access Controls

Community-based

Linked social accounts, verified memberships, or holders of specific items

Geographic Targeting

Exclude high-bot regions

Holders Benefit

Only holders of a specific item

Claim Limits

One-per-person limits with wallet verification

Distribution Modes

Mass Share: One QR code for social media (with protections)
Unique Codes: Individual QR codes for events, trackable per person
Hybrid: Mix of both approaches for maximum control

Branded Experience

Custom Claim Pages:

Creator branding and messaging
Item previews and availability counter
Clear claiming instructions
Social sharing integration
