NFT Marketplace Offers

Bridging the pricing gap between buyers and sellers to unlock marketplace potential and drive transaction volume growth.

TEAM

TIMELINE

The Problem

The Challenge

Our NFT marketplace had a fundamental pricing disconnect. While we had plenty of listings, most were priced too high to sell. Sellers, especially newcomers, lacked market insight to price competitively.

Floor prices only showes the lowest listed price not what actually sold. This led to stagnant inventory and frustrated users on both sides.

Key Behavioral Insights

We observed several patterns through user behavior analysis:

High Listing Volume

Lots of NFTs listed but only few sells

Seller Confusion

New sellers lack understanding of good pricing

Failed Auctions

Good bid activity existed but rarely met minimum thresholds

Transaction History Evidence

Clear evidence that sales happened when prices aligned with market demand, but the disconnect between listing prices and actual market value was creating a stagnant marketplace.

Research & Discovery

Understanding the Market

With a tight timeline, we focused on competitive analysis and behavioral data. I studied how leading platforms like OpenSea, Magic Eden, and OKX handled price discovery.

User Journey: Finding the Right Price

Key Insight

Sellers need market feedback to price competitively, and offers provide real-time demand.

Solution Design

The Solution

We introduced a comprehensive offers system that would bridge the pricing gap between buyers and sellers, providing market signals and enabling price discovery.

Core Features

Visible Market Intent

All offers displayed prominently on NFT pages

Time-bound Offers

Expiration dates create urgency and clarity

Smart Notifications

Alerts for new offers, expirations, and acceptance

One-click Acceptance

Streamlined flow for sellers to accept offers
